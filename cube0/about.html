ABOUT

    WHAT IS THIS?

    CUBE0 is a 3D voxel game developed by deniskincses, using CST1229's 3D extension. It is inspired by Notch's older versions of Minecraft, with extras added. Develompent started at April 21st, 2024.

    WHEN IS IT LAUNCHING?

    The game is still in its development stages, so frankly I can't say a precise date.

    WHERE IS IT GOING TO BE PLAYABLE?

    The first alpha stages of the game will only be playable on your browser, because I do not know how to implement DRM into my games, and since the game cannot be reversed engineered if it's in a website, then it will only be available there.

    WHAT WILL BE THE FUTURE OF THIS GAME?

    I cannot say for sure, as I just started a week ago, but I will be definetly adding more stuff into this game, such as a cave system, survival, enemies, ores, crafting system, biomes, and definetly more dimensions.
